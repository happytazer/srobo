SMARS

WHAT IS SMARS?

A simple robot designed to the educational field. The main features of SMARS are the reduced number of components, the possibility of mount it without screws and without soldering any part, it doesnt need glue or similar and its really cheap to build. Another main point of SMARS is the modular design. You can mount different sensors and tools and design your own parts.

Material required to make this SMARS

  1. N20 gear motors

  2. 9v Battery

  3. Arduino uno

  4. Motor driver sheild

  5. Bluetooth Module Hc-05

  6. Ultra sonic device

  7. 3d Printed Body of robot (file avilable in the github)
